Eligible vehicles

From: Transport Canada

If you are looking to lease or purchase a new zero-emission vehicle and would like to know whether it is eligible for the Incentives for Zero-Emission Vehicles (iZEV) Program incentive, please consult the List of eligible vehicles under the iZEV Program below. To help with your eligible vehicles search, you can sort each column alphabetically (both ascending and descending), including by:

  • eligible model year(s)
  • make, model and trim
  • fuel type, and electric range; and
  • the maximum available incentive for each vehicle (up to $5,000).

NOTICE: The Incentives for Zero-Emission Vehicles (iZEV) Program is continuing until March 31, 2025 (or until available funding is exhausted).

Please note that we update the iZEV Program’s list of eligible vehicles whenever a new make and/or model is approved.

If you have any questions, please contact us at iZEV-iVZE@tc.gc.ca.

If you have any additional questions about a particular make or model, please contact the auto manufacturer or your local dealership.

Important note

This list will be updated on a regular basis; please check to make sure the car you are interested in purchasing is on the list. Please consult automakers' websites for more precise and detailed information and specifications for the vehicles on this list.

All vehicles that appear on the list have been deemed eligible under the iZEV program, based on manufacturer's suggested retail prices (MSRPs) provided to Transport Canada by automakers. For the purposes of this program, MSRP excludes additional costs, such as freight, delivery, and other fees. In Quebec, regulations requires merchants to advertise an "all inclusive" price, which includes these additional costs.

Eligibility, including the continuation of eligibility, remains at the Minister of Transport's discretion.

All fees and costs related to the purchase or lease of an eligible vehicles, such as freight and delivery charges, should be comparable to those same fees and costs for other vehicles sold by the same automaker. If Transport Canada assesses that an automaker is potentially departing from standard pricing approaches to these fees/costs, Transport Canada reserves the right to withdraw a vehicle from the eligibility list.
